The Death Star

The Death Star is a fictional mobile megastructure and galactic superweapon appearing in the Star Wars science-fiction franchise created by George Lucas. Crewed with an estimated population of 1.7 million military personnel and 400,000 droids,[1] this dwarf planet-sized fortress was designed with extremely powerful power projection capabilities, capable of destroying an entire planet with one blast from its superlaser.
