trash preparation

  • bag, tie, and replace your trash in the 13 gallon container provided
  • no loose trash
  • double bag all pet waste
  • no bags over 20 lbs
  • no broken glass or sharp objects
  • no oversized items i.e. furniture, large boxes, etc.

your container

  • valet waste provides each residence with one 13 gallon trash container
  • place your container with tied trash bag outside of your door between 6pm and 8pm on scheduled service nights
  • collection will begin after 8pm

evening collection

  • bring your container back inside by 9am
  • containers left out during the day may result in interruption of your service
  • no service on: 4th of july, labor day, thanksgiving, christmas eve/day, new years eve/day
